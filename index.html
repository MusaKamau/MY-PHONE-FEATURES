Awesome Features

Display

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 has a 7.6-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with a 120Hz refresh rate. The outer display is a 6.2-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with a 120Hz refresh rate.

Processor

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 for Galaxy processor.

RAM

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 has 12GB of RAM. Storage: The Galaxy Z Fold 5 has 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB of internal storage.

Camera

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 has a triple-lens rear camera system with a 50-megapixel main sensor, a 12-megapixel ultrawide sensor, and a 10-megapixel telephoto sensor. It also has a 10-megapixel front-facing camera.

Battery

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 has a 4,400mAh battery with support for 25W wired charging and 15W wireless charging.

Software

The Galaxy Z Fold 5 runs Android 13.
